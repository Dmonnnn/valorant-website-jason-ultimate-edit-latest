Agents

Agents are the playable characters in VALORANT, representing an agent of the VALORANT Protocol. Each agent serves as a different class with four abilities and are mostly unlocked by progressing through their Contract. Agents are experts in slicing up dangerous territory to set their team up for success. agents are self-sufficient fraggers who their team expects, through abilities and skills, to get high frags and seek out engagements first.
